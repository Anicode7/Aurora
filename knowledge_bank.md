## Company Vision & Mission

**Vision:** To make confident English communication accessible to every Indian — regardless of city, background, or education.

**Mission:** SpeakX helps Hindi and regional language speakers in Tier 1, 2, and 3 cities transform from "I understand English but can't speak it" to fluent, confident communicators — through daily, guided speaking practice with our AI teacher Sia.

We believe language confidence is a career multiplier. A working professional who can speak English fluently earns 30–40% more, gets promoted faster, and commands respect in interviews and meetings. We exist to close that gap — one conversation at a time.

## North Star Metric

**Primary North Star:** Monthly Retention  
**Definition:** The percentage of trial users who convert to a monthly paid plan AND complete at least one exercise within that month.  
**Formula:** `(trial → monthly converters who complete ≥1 exercise) / (total trial users) × 100`  
**Current Benchmark:** 34% (target: 45% by Q3)

**Supporting Metrics:**
- **Trial → Monthly Conversion Rate:** % of trial users who upgrade within 7 days (current: 22%, target: 30%)
- **W1 Retention:** % of new paid users who complete an exercise in their first week post-conversion (current: 58%, target: 72%)
- **D30 Streak Rate:** % of paid users who maintain a streak of ≥5 days in their first month (current: 41%)
- **Referral Rate:** % of paid users who invite at least one friend (current: 8%, target: 15%)

## Target Audience Profiles

### Persona 1 — The Ambitious Professional (Primary)
- **Age:** 24–35 | **Cities:** Tier 2 & Tier 3 (Lucknow, Indore, Bhopal, Patna, Surat)
- **Background:** Working in sales, operations, BPO, or mid-level corporate roles
- **Pain Point:** Understands English but freezes when speaking; passed over for promotions due to communication gap
- **Motivation:** Career growth, salary hike, social confidence in professional settings
- **App Usage Pattern:** Prefers 10–15 min sessions, active between 7–9 AM and 8–10 PM
- **Churn Trigger:** Feels embarrassed making mistakes; quits after first week slump

### Persona 2 — The College Student (Secondary)
- **Age:** 18–24 | **Cities:** Tier 1 & Tier 2 (Delhi NCR, Pune, Hyderabad, Jaipur)
- **Background:** Engineering or commerce undergraduate; preparing for placements or competitive exams
- **Pain Point:** Group discussions and HR rounds in English; peer pressure around fluency
- **Motivation:** Campus placements, social status among peers, cracking interviews
- **App Usage Pattern:** Irregular — binge sessions before exams, drops off in semester breaks
- **Churn Trigger:** Novelty wears off after 2 weeks; needs social accountability

### Persona 3 — The Homemaker Upskiller (Emerging)
- **Age:** 28–42 | **Cities:** Tier 2 & Tier 3
- **Background:** Educated homemaker re-entering the workforce or improving for children's schooling
- **Pain Point:** Self-consciousness in parent-teacher meetings, social gatherings, online work
- **Motivation:** Self-improvement, respect, helping children with English
- **App Usage Pattern:** Afternoon sessions (1–3 PM), highly consistent once habit is formed
- **Churn Trigger:** Feels the app is "not for her"; needs warm, non-judgemental tone

## Product Features

### Core Learning
- **Sia — AI Speaking Partner:** Real-time conversational AI that simulates job interviews, customer calls, daily conversations, and debate practice. Provides instant feedback on pronunciation, grammar, fluency, and confidence score.
- **Role-Based Scenarios:** 50+ speaking scenarios across job interviews, office meetings, customer service, travel, and social situations — updated monthly.
- **Pronunciation Engine:** Word-level feedback with native speaker audio comparison; tracks improvement over sessions.
- **Daily Speaking Drills:** 5–10 minute micro-sessions designed to build habit without overwhelming the user.

### Gamification
- **Streak System:** Daily streak counter with milestone rewards at 7, 14, 30, and 60 days. Broken streaks can be "saved" with a Streak Shield (purchasable with coins).
- **Coins Economy:** Users earn coins for completing sessions, maintaining streaks, and achieving milestones. Coins redeem for Streak Shields, bonus scenarios, and premium content unlocks.
- **Leaderboard:** Weekly leaderboard ranked by exercises completed and fluency score improvement. Segmented into friend leaderboard and global leaderboard.
- **Badges & Achievements:** 40+ achievement badges for milestones (First Conversation, 10-Day Streak, Fluency Level Up, Top 10% This Week, etc.)

### AI Companion
- **Sia Personality:** Warm, patient, encouraging. Never judges mistakes — frames errors as "learning moments." Adapts difficulty based on user's current fluency score.
- **Personalized Learning Path:** Based on onboarding quiz (goal, current level, available time), Sia generates a 30-day learning roadmap.
- **Progress Intelligence:** Weekly fluency score with before/after comparison. Monthly report card with session count, streak history, top improvement areas, and peer benchmarking.

### Social & Community
- **Friend Challenges:** Users can challenge friends to weekly speaking duels — most exercises completed wins.
- **Group Leaderboards:** Office or college group leaderboards for friendly competition.
- **Referral Program:** Refer a friend → both get 7-day premium extension.

## Ethical Communication Guidelines

### Allowed Tones
- **Motivational** — Forward-looking, progress-focused, energising
- **Encouraging** — Warm, supportive, celebrates small wins without being patronising
- **Celebratory** — Acknowledges milestones and achievements genuinely
- **Urgent (mild)** — Creates gentle time pressure without panic; "don't miss this" framing
- **Friendly** — Conversational, peer-like, casual but respectful
- **Informative** — Clear, factual, useful — used for progress updates and feature discovery

### Disallowed Tones
- **Shaming** — Never reference what the user hasn't done in a way that makes them feel bad about themselves
- **Aggressive sales pressure** — No hard-sell, no "BUY NOW OR LOSE EVERYTHING" framing
- **Fear-mongering** — Do not exaggerate negative consequences of not using the app
- **Condescending** — Never imply the user is slow, lazy, or behind others in a demeaning way

### Additional Communication Rules
- Never compare a user unfavourably to a named peer
- Always frame streaks and progress as "yours to protect" not "yours to lose"
- Hindi messages must be transcreated — not literally translated. Tone must feel native
- Maximum 1 push notification per time window; never more than 3 per day for low-activeness segments
- Win-back messages must lead with value already received, not with what was missed

## User Journey Stages

### Trial — Days 0–7 (Activation Window)
**Goal:** Convert to paid subscriber  
**Critical Moment:** Day 1 (first Sia conversation = "aha moment"), Day 6–7 (conversion push)  
**Key Hooks:** Scarcity (trial ending), Accomplishment (first win), Epic Meaning (career transformation)  
**Success Signal:** User completes ≥3 sessions AND views progress report before Day 7

### Paid — Days 8–30 (Habit Formation Window)
**Goal:** Build daily practice habit; drive W1 Retention and D30 Streak  
**Critical Moment:** Day 8–10 (validate purchase), Day 21–25 (mid-subscription slump)  
**Key Hooks:** Loss Avoidance (streak protection), Social Influence (leaderboard), Accomplishment (milestones)  
**Success Signal:** User maintains ≥4 sessions/week by end of Day 30

### Retention — Days 31+ (Loyalty Window)
**Goal:** Drive renewal and referral behaviour  
**Critical Moment:** Days 29–30 (renewal decision), Day 45+ (referral prompt)  
**Key Hooks:** Ownership (progress owned), Epic Meaning (long-term transformation), Unpredictability (new content)  
**Success Signal:** User renews AND refers ≥1 friend within 60 days

### Churned — Day 31+ post-cancellation (Win-Back Window)
**Goal:** Re-activate with nostalgia and time-limited offer  
**Critical Moment:** Day 3, Day 7, Day 14 post-churn (diminishing returns after Day 14)  
**Key Hooks:** Loss Avoidance (saved progress waiting), Scarcity (40% off expires), Epic Meaning (unfinished journey)  
**Success Signal:** User reopens app and completes 1 session within 7 days of win-back notification

### Inactive — Paid but no session in 7+ days (Re-engagement Window)
**Goal:** Trigger one micro-action with zero friction  
**Critical Moment:** Day 7 of inactivity (before habit fully breaks)  
**Key Hooks:** Unpredictability (new challenge/feature), Social Influence (friend activity), Loss Avoidance (streak at risk)  
**Success Signal:** User completes any in-app action within 48 hours of notification
